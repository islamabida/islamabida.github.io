About the Page


Founded on 4th July of 2009 in New York,United States.Explore is a trusted community marketplace for people to list ,discover and book unique accommodations around the world.Explore connects people to unique travel experiences at any price point, more than 47,000 cities and 170 countries.With best world-class customer service ,Explore is the easiest way for people to monetize their extra space and showcase it to an audience of millions.


Our travel guides, which are curated by Travel plus Leisure editors and a network of local correspondents, highlight the best hotels, restaurants, shops, bars, and things to do in the world's most exciting destinations. Browse our in-depth travel information for great ideas and insider finds, plus smart travel tips that will have you feeling like a local in no time.

SERVICES

What we offer


Tourism Transportation

The Tourism Transportation Department of the Explore Tourism Company polices, investigates, intervenes and when necessary sanctions all persons and organizations providing ground transportation to visitors. These include vehicles such as taxis, excursion buses, shuttles, charter buses, limousines, among others.

Tour Guide

Our tour guides are professionals and they offer not only information, but enhance the image of a destination as well as the way a visitor experiences a location or attraction.

Travel Agents

We offer advice on transportation, itineraries, accommodations and details pertaining to travel.Our tourism transportation department oversees travel agencies’ compliance regarding their regulatory obligations and serves as an arbiter when customer issues arise.
